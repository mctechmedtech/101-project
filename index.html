Hi, I'm a developer that loves clean & elegant code.

A little bit about me.

I've made my home base in Chester County, Pennsylvania with my family. My journey into tech started with a degree in biology followed by a degree in medical technology. During my years as a clinical laboratory scientist in a community hospital I became acutely aware of the importance of maintaining one's health and became very interested in nutrition, fitness and community: the pillars of optimal health. My research led me to a world of like-minded individuals who are trying to improve the health and wellness of as many people as possible through nutrition and prevention.

I wanted to learn how to build my own site in order to share all of the valuable information that I've acquired and to help others in the wellness community do the same. I learned to code and now I'm hooked! It opened a new door. When I learned to build my first website, I realized that I had found something that would provide freedom & versatility in my work and it's something that I love to do! Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
